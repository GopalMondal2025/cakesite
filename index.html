About Delight Cake

Discover our journey of passion, craftsmanship, and delicious creations

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Delight Cake began with a simple vision: to create extraordinary cakes that bring joy to every celebration. What started as a small home-based operation has blossomed into a beloved bakery trusted by hundreds of families across Kolkata.

Our founder, Sarah, grew up in a family where every special occasion was marked by homemade desserts. This childhood inspiration ignited her passion for baking and led to the creation of Delight Cake. Today, we continue that tradition with modern techniques and premium ingredients.

Every cake we bake is a labor of love, carefully crafted to ensure that each bite is a moment of pure delight. We believe that the best cakes are made with quality ingredients, creativity, and a genuine love for what we do.
